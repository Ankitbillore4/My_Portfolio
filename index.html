About Me

I'm Ankit Billore a dedicated front-end developer with a strong background in creating fully responsive and visually engaging websites. With experience from a recent internship at Skill Craft Technology and projects like a responsive clothes website, I've honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern front-end frameworks. My work reflects a passion for user-centered design and performance-focused coding.

Currently, I am expanding my skills by diving into the MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) stack through Sheryians Coding School. This training allows me to build dynamic, full-stack applications, and I'm excited about the opportunities to apply these new skills in real-world projects.
